    Description
    Complete your home or small office with the Staples Kendall Park Cherry Double Pedestal desk in a traditional cherry laminate finish The Staples Kendall Park double pedestal desk features a spacious workspace with optimal storage for your home or small office
    • Double pedestal desk
    • Laminated Cherry finish
    • One letter file sized drawers
    • Three additional drawers for additional storage
    • Additional cabinet for storage of technology or supplies
    • Overall Dimensions: 30" H x 58.5" W x 23.2" D
    • 1-Year Limited Warranty
    • Full assembly required
    • Product Instructions
    Update your small office or home office with the Quill Brand Kendall Park double pedestal desk. Enjoy ample storage without compromising precious space. Stay organized with 1 file sized drawer perfect for letter sized documents or use for any other storage needs, keep your other office supplies close at hand in three additional drawers. Tuck away additional electronics or office supplies in the additional cabinet space. The dark cherry laminate adds timeless style to any small office space. The back of the desk is finished allowing for unlimited options for configuration in your room of choice. Open grommet holes let you pass wires and power cords through, so you can charge devices without experiencing tangled cables on your workspace.
